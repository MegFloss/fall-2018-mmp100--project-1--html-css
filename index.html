Elliott Smith

Elliott Smith a Singer-Songwriter best known for such works as Miss Misery, Needle and Hay and Say Yes. Born in Omaha, NE on August 6, 1969, he lived in Texas till his teenage years when he moved to Portland, OR. He gain noteriety for his work Good Will Hunting's soundtrack, for which his song Miss Misery was nominated. Elliott had a rare ability to write compelling lyrics with harmonies that were complex, but kept a delicate tembre. Elliott Smith is interesting to me because because his songs feel like an endless labyrinth, everytime you feel like you have him figured out you find another door.
