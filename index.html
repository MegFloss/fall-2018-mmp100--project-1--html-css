Scott Ramon Seguro Mescudi who is better known for his stage name Kid Cudi is a recording artist from Cleveland, Ohio. This man is one of the most versatile person in the music industry. Although he does not chase commercial success there are many who he has inspired. Kid Cudi is one of my favorite creative of all time his music is special to me because he was never afraid to be himself. His music helped me understand things about myself such as emotions and how to express them.
