Elon Musk - Engineer, Leader and Visionary

Elon Musk is many things. He is a CEO and founder of two of the most revolutionary companies in the modern world, Tesla and SpaceX. He is a genius engineer, constantly pushing the boundaries of what is possible in Science and Technology. And he is a true visionary, foreseeing worlds others cannot even imagine.
Elon came from Pretoria, South Africa and was coding since the young ripe age of 10 years old. He than made his way to Canada as a teen and than came to the United States to finish college.
He than became an extremely successful entrepreneur starting many web companies during a time when the internet was still in its infancy. This made him very rich and allowed him to pursue his visions for humanity, starting Tesla, a company meant to bring us into an electric car future, and SpaceX, a company meant to bring us to mars and beyond.
Elon's mission, work ethic, and drive are so impressive to me. As an aspiring engineer and entrepreneur myself, he is a true inspiration to me! There are very few people on this planet like Elon and I hope that I can acomplish even a small part of what he was able to.
