Celebrate the Bday of Hollywood Actor William Holden

Born in O'Fallon Illinois, William Holden was the son of a small business owner. Holden himself became a business man and philanthropist. The great Hollywood movie legend William Holden was more like a business man portraying an actor than an actor portraying himself. Born William Franklin Beedle, Bill Holden was the son of an entrepreneur and in many respects would have rather been known for the legacy of his innovative thinking as an entrepreneur than his craft as a great actor in the movies. Though determined to do the best at whatever his endeavor, Bill Beedle set a standard for integrity,success and hard work.

The 1950 classic Sunset Blvd. starring Bill Holden Gloria Swanson was a true and honest story about the popular culture of Hollywood. Nearly all the actors in the film portrayed snippets of their authentic life story. Swanson having been a washed up film star from the "silent movie era"and Holden instead of being a struggling script writer was at the time a struggling actor having difficulty finding good paying and respectful roles that is until he found "it", the blockbuster film noir in Sunset Blvd.
