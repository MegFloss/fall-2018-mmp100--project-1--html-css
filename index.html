Donald Glover

Donald glover is an actor, comedian, singer, writer, producer, director, rapper, and DJ

The man is a talented visionary capable of bringing creativity to his work that no one has seen before. Two of my favorite pieces of work that he has produced is the Atlanta TV show on FX and my favorite album Because The Internet

Because the Internet

Top 5 Favorite songs from Because the Internet

  1. 1. Death by Numbers
  2. 2. III. Life the Biggest Troll
  3. 3. 3005
  4. 4. I. Flight of the Navigator
  5. 5. Telegraph Ave.

Because the Internet is the first album to introduce me to Donald Glovers Childish Gambino alter ego. I started listening to Childish Gambio at around freshman year of college in City Tech. He was one of the artists that helped me through rough times. There are other individual songs that he has made that have their own greatness to them but nothing could ever beat this album.
